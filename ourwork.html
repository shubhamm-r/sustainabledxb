    • Our Work!
    Check out all the work and events we have done with our wonderful volunteers!
    Our Book-A Journey Through the SDGs!
    A guide on the SDGs, written by our Sustainable Team members! How cool! The book was released on Amazon on the 4th of July for everyone to pick up and learn more about the SDGs.
    Our Collaboration with Neutral Fuels!
    The Sustainable Team was able to facilitate a partnership between STS, the school bus provider, and Neutral Fuels, who specialise in environmentally-friendly fuel solutions. With this partnership, our school has completely shifted to Neutral Fuels for their bus fuel needs!
    Poll Bins-Make your waste count!
    Want to make your waste worthwhile? Use it as a vote in our poll bins! Each poll bin has a question to which you can choose your side by disposing of your waste in your preferred choice of the bin. Make your choice count!
    Our First Newspaper Drive!
    In The Sustainable Team’s first newspaper drive, our volunteers were able to collect 657kgs of newspaper in less than 2 hours! That’s 5kgs per minute! This would not have been possible without our marvellous and laborious volunteers!
    Sign Language Workshop
    In collaboration with the Social Outreach and the Wellness Council, we held a virtual sign language workshop led by Ms Gulshan Kavarana. It was an enriching session for all students, who were now able to make basic conversation using sign language.
    Sustainable Families!
    The Sustainable Families program was created to involve not just the students but their families in playing their role to achieve one SDG of their choice. Each family linked themselves to one goal and perform various innotive activities revolving around the goal thereby playing their part as a family to achieve the desired goal!
