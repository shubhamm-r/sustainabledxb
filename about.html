    • Who are we?
    We are more than just your regular environmental organisation! The Sustainable Team began in 2019 by a few enthusiastic grade 11 students at GEMS Modern Academy to play their part in accomplishing the Sustainable Development Goals set out by the United Nations(That's where our name comes from!). Therefore, we don't just talk about the environment! We look to solve humanitarian issues such as poverty and hunger- one step at a time. With our incredible team and wonderful volunteers, we have been able to grow from spreading awareness on social media to making changes in our community!
    Our Aims
    Our overarching aim is to play our role in the fulfilment of the 17 Sustainable Development Goals, no matter how big or small. Sustainability just doesn't refer to the maintenance of the environment or nature. It also refers to humanitarian issues such as poverty and education. We aim to educate people about sustainable living by raising awareness about the SDGs and the importance of taking part in fulfilling them. Therefore, join us and play your part!
